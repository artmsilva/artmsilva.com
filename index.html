Art Silva works at Lowe's in Creative Services as a front-end developer. From time to time he picks up the odd freelance project too. Aside from client work, projects include the development blog Development by Me and selling themes & templates at Superhug.

He writes in 140 characters at Twitter, posts photos to Instagram, creates 400x300px works-in-progress on Dribbble, listens to all his music on Rdio and answers the occasional question on Quora.

A Mexican American, Art has lived on both coasts and traveled through the South. He currently lives in Charlotte, North Carolina, a place where he feels life gets better each day.
