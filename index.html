Art Silva is a freelance web designer and front-end developer. Aside from client work, projects include the development blog Development by Me and the WordPress portfolio theme, Gabby.

He writes about design, apps and life at Posts, writes in 140 characters at Twitter, posts photos to Instagram, creates 400x300px works-in-progress on Dribbble, listens to all his music on Rdio and answers the occasional question on Quora.

A Mexican American, Art has lived on both coasts and traveled through the South. He currently lives in Charlotte, North Carolina, a place where he feels life gets better each day.
